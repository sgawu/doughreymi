About

Welcome to Dough Rey Mi, my name is Sophia! I was born in Accra, Ghana where I was fortunate enough to have a grandma who was a baker. I often woke up to the smell of fresh bread and the sound of pans clanging. She has been my inspiration for many things but I especially owe her for my love of baking! This page is the first step to realzing my dream of one day following in her footsteps and owning my own bakery.


Join me; let's bake and break bread together....
